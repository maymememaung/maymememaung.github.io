  • A Web Platform for analyzing fairness of newly proposed congressional district plans for different states in the US

    A web-based client-server system to analyze political fairness of the proposed district plans drawn after 2020 US Census, through visualization of a variety of standard measures. This team project requires performing multi-dimensional analysis of plans with high performance computing cluster, and displaying them through a user-friendly and interactive website.

  • A Web Platform for Showcasing Historical Postcards

    The goal of this project is to develop a web-based platform to display the stories of people employed in domestic labor in the British Empire through a variety of archival postcards. This project, a collaboration with a history professor and her students from the College of New Jersey, is supervised by Professor Joydeep from Stony Brook University.
